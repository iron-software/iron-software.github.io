    Class GeneratedBarcode

    A barcode generated by IronBarcode.

    In addition to exporting the barcode as a graphic, we may also resize it, recolor it and annotate it.

    GeneratedBarcode may be exported as an image, object, or PDF file. It may also be exported as HTML for use on the web.
